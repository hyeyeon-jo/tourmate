Welcome to Daejeon

As the largest city in Chungcheong Province, it is the largest science and research city in Korea and one of the world's leading science and research cities, with Daedeok R&D Special Zone (Daedeok Research Complex) and the International Science Business Belt being established..

Top Attractions in Daejeon

Sky Road

Located in Daejeon City, the Sky Road was one of the city's landmarks, a walking trail with beautiful scenery. It is especially famous for being able to enjoy the fantastic night view at night.

Daedong Sky Park

The murals decorated all over the village and painted from house to house make it a wonderful photo zone, which is good for taking pictures and enjoying a walk.

Daejeon O-World

The name "Oh World" means a space where people feel and move the surprise and joy of the exclamation "Oh!" Over the past 20 years, children and teenagers in Daejeon and surrounding areas have made happy memories here.

Daejeon's Best Local Dishes

"Sungsimdang". Bakery

Daejeon is famous for bread, especially this bakery is the most famous and delicious place in Daejeon.

tteokbokki

There are a lot of famous tteokbokki restaurants, and the tteokbokki restaurant called "The Restaurant(바로그집)" is the most famous.

Travel Tips for Daejeon

  • Best Time to Visit: Spring (April to June) and autumn (September to November) provide mild weather, making it suitable for outdoor activities because there are many markets and simple snacks.
  • Transportation: Since there are not many subway stations, we recommend traveling by bus. The area is not that wide, making it suitable for traveling on foot.
  • Festivals: Daejeon is famous for bread, so many tourists visit it in time for the bread festival.
